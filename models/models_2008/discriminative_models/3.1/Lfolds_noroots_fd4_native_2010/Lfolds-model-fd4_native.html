Lfolds-model-fd4_native and Rfolds-model-fd4_native

These models are for 3.1 graphs which include cortical thickness information. They may be used for morphometry, but not for sulcal recognition yet because they have not been trained on a suitable database.

These models are the same as the folds-model-fd4 models, except that they provide measurements in the native space of subjects rather than in the normalized Talairach space.
