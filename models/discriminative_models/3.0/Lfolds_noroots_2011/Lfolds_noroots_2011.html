The 2011 ANN models are a just mode modern variant of the older 2000-2001 models, which differ on the following points:

  • They are using SVR models (regression SVM, support vector machines) instead of MLP (multi-layer perceptrons), which provide sensibly the same performance but are easier to handle in our machine learning framework.
  • They have been trained on the 2008 learning database of 62 subjects, which means that they are more accurate, and follow the new sulci nomenclature, like the SPAM-based recognition models.

    • In other respects, they are similar to the 2000-2001 models.
